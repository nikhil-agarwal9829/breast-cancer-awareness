Turmeric Golden Milk

A powerful anti-inflammatory drink that can help reduce inflammation and boost immunity.

  • Heat 2 cups of milk of choice
  • Add 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • Add pinch of black pepper
  • Optional: honey to taste

Ginger Tea

Natural remedy for nausea and digestive discomfort during treatment.

  • Slice fresh ginger root
  • Boil in water for 10 minutes
  • Strain and add lemon
  • Add honey if desired

Chamomile Tea

Helps with sleep and anxiety during treatment.

  • Use one tea bag per cup
  • Steep for 5 minutes
  • Drink 30 mins before bed
  • Add honey if desired
