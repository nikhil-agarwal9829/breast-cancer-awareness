Nutrition Guide

Healthy eating tips and meal plans for breast cancer patients and survivors

General Nutrition Tips

  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water
  • Eat a variety of colorful fruits and vegetables
  • Choose whole grains over refined grains
  • Include lean protein in your diet
  • Limit processed foods and added sugars
  • Eat smaller, more frequent meals if needed
  • Listen to your body's hunger and fullness cues

Fruits and Vegetables

Rich in antioxidants and essential nutrients.

  • Berries (blueberries, strawberries)
  • Leafy greens (spinach, kale)
  • Cruciferous vegetables (broccoli, cauliflower)
  • Citrus fruits (oranges, grapefruits)

Lean Proteins

Essential for muscle maintenance and recovery.

  • Fish (salmon, tuna)
  • Poultry (chicken, turkey)
  • Legumes (beans, lentils)
  • Eggs and dairy

Whole Grains

Provide sustained energy and fiber.

  • Brown rice
  • Quinoa
  • Oats
  • Whole wheat bread

Healthy Fats

Support overall health and nutrient absorption.

  • Avocados
  • Nuts and seeds
  • Olive oil
  • Fatty fish

Sample Meal Plan

  • Breakfast: Oatmeal with berries and nuts
  • Mid-morning snack: Greek yogurt with honey
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken salad with avocado
  • Afternoon snack: Mixed nuts and dried fruit
  • Dinner: Baked salmon with quinoa and vegetables
  • Evening snack: Fresh fruit or herbal tea
