Exercise Guide

Safe and effective exercises for breast cancer patients and survivors

Important Tips Before Starting

  • Always consult your healthcare provider before starting any exercise program
  • Start slowly and gradually increase intensity
  • Listen to your body and stop if you feel pain or discomfort
  • Stay hydrated during exercise
  • Wear comfortable, supportive clothing
  • Exercise in a well-ventilated area
  • Include warm-up and cool-down periods
Easy

Walking

A low-impact exercise that helps improve circulation and maintain mobility.

  • Start with 5-10 minutes
  • Gradually increase to 30 minutes
  • Maintain a comfortable pace
  • Use proper footwear
Easy

Gentle Stretching

Improves flexibility and helps prevent muscle stiffness.

  • Hold each stretch for 15-30 seconds
  • Don't bounce or force movements
  • Focus on breathing
  • Include neck and shoulder stretches
Medium

Gentle Yoga

Combines stretching, breathing, and relaxation techniques.

  • Start with basic poses
  • Use props for support
  • Focus on breathing
  • Avoid strenuous poses
Medium

Light Strength Training

Helps maintain muscle mass and improve overall strength.

  • Use light weights or resistance bands
  • Focus on proper form
  • Start with 8-10 repetitions
  • Include rest periods
