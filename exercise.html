Exercise Guide

Safe and effective exercises for breast cancer patients and survivors

Back to Features

Gentle Stretching

Start with gentle stretches to improve flexibility and reduce stiffness.

  • Shoulder rolls - 10 times each direction
  • Neck stretches - hold 15 seconds each side
  • Arm circles - 10 times each direction
  • Wrist rotations - 10 times each direction

Walking Program

A progressive walking program to build endurance safely.

  • Start with 5-10 minutes daily
  • Gradually increase to 30 minutes
  • Maintain comfortable pace
  • Walk on even surfaces

Modified Yoga

Gentle yoga poses to improve flexibility and reduce stress.

  • Cat-Cow Stretch
  • Child's Pose
  • Modified Downward Dog
  • Seated Twists

Exercise Safety Tips

Listen to Your Body

Start slowly and stop if you feel pain or discomfort.

Timing Matters

Exercise when your energy levels are highest.

Consult Your Doctor

Get medical clearance before starting any exercise program.
